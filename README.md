# LHS Life
The all-in-one companion app for La Salle High School, Yakima.

LHS Life is built to be the fastest way to find what you're looking for, whether that be:
- Checking when the next period is,
- Ordering lunch,
- Checking your grades,
- Viewing or submitting assignments,
- Creating a homework reminder for later,
- Receiving professional dress reminders the night before,
- Receiving ASB reminders to head to Student Store,
- Or catching an athletic event.

## What to expect

### 🔔 Bell schedule

LHS Life is always up-to-date with the official school calendar.
You can choose to display the next bell on your Lock Screen and Dynamic Island every day, abnormal days only (liturgy, early release, extended lunch, etc.), or never.
Upcoming events and bells will always be shown within the app.

For example, during the day you might see:

**| English in 3 min**

**|** *Until 8:50*

### 📚 Adding homework

Tap the homework button to open a quick popup, where you can add a title, due date, and priority (0–3) for your assignment.
LHS life will automatically label your task with the class you're currently sitting in and save to Apple Reminders.

### 🍴 PowerSchool, Schoology, and ordering lunch

LHS Life embeds these webpages directly in the app, so you don't have to chase websites or other applications for quick checks or tasks.
It's all the familiar apps you're used to, now all in one place.

### ✏️ Customization

Within LHS Life, you are free to name and color your periods, disable the ones you don't have (0 period, 8th period, etc.), and set your notification preferences.
LHS Life also allows students on ASB to enable Student Store and morning announcement reminders.

## How do I get LHS Life?
LHS Life is planned to launch for the **2026–2027** school year.

After launch, find us on the App Store!

### Supported platforms:
- iPad, iOS 17.0 or later
- iPhone, iOS 17.0 or later.

### Notes:
- LHS Life is available exclusively to LaSalle students.
- iOS 26.0 or later is recommend for all users.
- All of your personal data stays on your device. LHS Life has **NO** methods to view your private information.
